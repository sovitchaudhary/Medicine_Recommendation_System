About Hamro Hospital

Hamro Hospital, a private hospital with a public purpose, offers a world-class quaternary care facility, featuring multiple Centers of Excellence. It brings together the finest medical minds in the country and advanced technology to provide holistic treatment within a multi-disciplinary framework. As the first NABH International (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers) accredited hospital in Nepal, Hamro Hospital aspires to become the healthcare destination of choice in N. It provides a platform for practicing 'Safe and Evidence Based Medicine' driven by international guidelines and protocols. The hospital represents the best amalgamation of world-class technology and the best medical minds, ensuring exceptional care for all patients. With a capacity of 750 beds,

Hamro Hospital is the largest private hospital offering super-specialty treatment services in Nepal. It boasts well-equipped modular operation theatres with Hepa filters, integrated features, and a Gas Scavenging System, incorporating the latest available technology. These advanced facilities ensure that patients receive the highest standard of medical care in a safe and sterile environment.

Hamro Hospital emphasizes affordability, accessibility, advanced medicine, and personalized care in providing a comprehensive range of healthcare services to citizens within their locality and country. This approach significantly impacts the nation's economic growth by minimizing the outflow of foreign currency. By offering advanced medical treatments locally, the hospital ensures that patients do not have to seek expensive medical care abroad, thereby retaining valuable resources within the country. The hospital is well-equipped with both ground and air ambulance services, setting a benchmark in world-class pre-hospital care for patients across the country. These services are crucial in providing timely and efficient medical attention to those in need, further enhancing the hospital's reputation for excellence in healthcare.

Infrastructurte

Spanning across 15 acres with a capacity of 750 beds, Hamro Hospital is the largest private hospital in Nepal. It covers a built-up area of over 600,000 sq. ft. and offers super-specialty treatment services. Hamro Hospital is equipped with the best ground ambulance service as well as a heli-ambulance. With a rooftop helipad in its own building and a ground helipad for emergency landings, it is capable of evacuating patients from any part of the country.

Hamro Hospital Hospital features 17 state-of-the-art, well-equipped modular operation theatres with HEPA filters, integrated systems, and a gas scavenging system, all adhering to the latest NABH and JCI norms. It also holds a 20-inch flat panel Cath lab. For consultations, up to 60 OPDs are available across all specialties.

The hospital is equipped with the latest Siemens Skyra MRI (3.0 Tesla) for superior precision in radio diagnosis, along with World-Class Interventional Radiology Services and Best-in-class Laboratory and Diagnostic Services under one roof. The Laboratory Medicine Department features a fully automated ESR (the first in the country), a fully Automated Coagulation Testing System, and Automated Immunohistochemistry (IHC) for Cancer Diagnosis, a Frozen Section, an Apheresis Machine, a Cell Separator, Single Donor Platelet Capabilities in-house, and a fully set up POCT.

The hospital offers the largest critical care program in the country, with over 75 beds in various Intensive Care Units. The critical care wing is strategically located near the Operation Theatre complex, ensuring better connectivity and seamless care. To uphold the highest safety standards for patients and staff, the building management system is equipped with multi-tiered access control and electronic security systems, including integrated CCTVs throughout the facility. Additionally, the facility features the latest elevator modules, service corridors, negative pressure rooms, isolation units, central AC/heating mechanisms, and an advanced fire safety management system, all designed in accordance with international norms.
